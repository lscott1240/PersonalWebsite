    • Upsilon Pi Epsilon Honors Society

    Issued by Henson School of Science & Technology · Feb 2023

    UPE is an international honor society in computing and information disciplines. The mission of Salisbury University Chapter of UPE is to stimulate students' interest in computer science, promote high scholarship, and recognize academic excellence. The UPE SU chapter was established in Spring 2017. Computer science students are eligible for membership by completing at least 60 semester hours of undergraduate credit including 16 semester hours in the computer science courses, an overall grade point average of at least 3.0, a grade point average of at least 3.0 in all computer science coursework and must rank in the top 35 percent of computer science students at SU.
